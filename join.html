Become a Member

Join the EUCCA Community

Connect with fellow chemistry enthusiasts, participate in exciting events, gain hands-on experience, and boost your career prospects.

Why Join EUCCA?

  • Network with peers, faculty, and industry professionals.
  • Gain hands-on experience through workshops and lab sessions.
  • Attend exclusive industrial talks, seminars, and field trips.
  • Participate in research projects and national competitions.
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills.

Membership Details

Eligibility: Membership is open to all registered students of Egerton University, regardless of their course of study. We welcome anyone with an interest in chemistry!

Membership Fee: An annual fee of **KES 100** is required for registration and to support our activities.

Membership Certificate: A membership certificate is available upon request for an additional **KES 100**.

How to Join: Simply fill out the application form below. Our Secretary General will review your application and contact you with further details regarding payment and membership confirmation.
